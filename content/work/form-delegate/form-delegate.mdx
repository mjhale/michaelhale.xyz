Form Delegate is a form handling platform built with a focus on privacy and security. It
allows users to encrypt messages with a public key via Keybase's KBPGP library, while also
providing convenient integrations with services such as SendGrid and Ifttt.

It is a service in active development that uses Elixir to power the backend. It is
capable of processing thousands of messages per minute on just a 200 MB memory Gigalixir
instance.
